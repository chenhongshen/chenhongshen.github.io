Hello there, and welcome to my homepage!

My name is Hongshen Chen (陈宏申), and I am a research scientist with Data Science Lab, JD.com. I also serve as a Youth Committee of CCF TCCI (Technical Committee of Chinese Information, China Computer Federation). I received my Ph.D. from NLP Group , Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Qun Liu. Prior to that, I receieved my B.E. from Sichuan University in 2012 and worked as a short-term research assistant at Singapore University of Technology and Design with Prof. Yue Zhang , 2016. Currently, I am focusing on natural language processing, dialogue systems, machine translation, sequence labelling, etc. I won best paper award on CCL 2016.

I maintain a list of my publications under the Publications tab, and a list of projects under the Projects tab. You may also check out my Google Scholar profile.

To get a better insight on my life so far, you can have a look at my curriculum vitae. I blog (although quite less frequently) and pin them down here.
